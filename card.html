Lemur

Lemurs are nocturnal and spend most of their lives in trees.

  • Location: Madagascar
  • Diet: Fruits and berries
  • Average weight: 6 pounds
  • Lifespan: 18 years

Lemurs originate from Mdagascar and the surrounding areas. They are generally social creatures. There are over 100 existing species of lemurs. Most lemurs have large eyes, long tails and a pointed snout.
